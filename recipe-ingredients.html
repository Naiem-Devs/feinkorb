Indian Dahl

4.8/5

2 Reviews

This simple Indian recipe is a must for anyone looking for a tasty curry, soup or stew that is vegan and high in protein.

preparation

  • Heat coconut oil in a saucepan and add chopped onions. Fry the onions until they are translucent (2-3 min). Then add chopped garlic and spices.
  • Now you can put the red lentils in the pot, which you washed with cold water beforehand. Now pour in your vegetable stock and stir until it starts to simmer. Let it simmer for 8-10 minutes until the lentils have absorbed most of the liquid.
  • Stir in the coconut milk and the strained tomatoes. Stir everything together and simmer for another 5-10 min until the lentils are tender (if the sauce is too thick, add more coconut milk). Season with salt, pepper and coconut sugar and add lime juice to taste.

Ingredients for

People

  • 1-2 tablespoons Organic coconut oil
  • 2 out of stock Onion
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon Turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander
  • 1 tablespoon Cumin
  • 1 tablespoon Red pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon Garam Masala
  • 1 2/3 cup (300g) Red Lentils
  • 3 1/4 cup (780g) vegetable stock
  • 1 cup (240ml) Coconut oil
  • 1 cup (240ml) Strained tomatoes
  • salt & pepper salt & Pepper
  • 2-3 tablespoons Organic coconut sugar
