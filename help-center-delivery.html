Delivery

We deliver your groceries to your home from Monday to Saturday. The opening times are varies and depend on the grocery store you order.

We deliver your groceries with with a delivery cost of 2.50€. Of course, you can also pick up your groceries free of charge from the grocery store by yourself.

Yes, the minimum order value is 15€ (private and corporate customers). Order everything you need, diverse and high quality.. And because this can sometimes be more, sometimes less, our minimum order value remains permanently low.

No - If you are not at home, we will leave your groceries at the location you specify (in front of your flat door, garage, neighbour:in etc.). If there are several people living in your house, the most common and convenient location is "in front of the flat door". You don't need to worry - your neighbours will let us in and we can usually get into the house. We will always find a solution to deliver your fresh mail, even if you are not at home.

Our delivery is environmentally friendly with electric bikes.

Our rider will ring the doorbell, leave the box at the front door and step back.

Depending on your order preference, you will receive a chat message from our rider for an alternative product.

If you receive alternative products or if something is missing, we will inform you by chat as soon as your order is packed. This way, it remains transparent for you which products you will receive and you are forewarned if an item is cancelled without replacement.

With alternative products, we make sure that the product is as close as possible to what you ordered. If your alternative product is cheaper, we will credit you with the difference in price.
